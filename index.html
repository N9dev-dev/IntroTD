About Me

I was born in Al Ain in 1992 and I still live and work there.

I began my career as a documentary photographer in 2009 and have since focused on portraiture. I participated in many projects with the Department of Culture and Tourism - Abu Dhabi and Abu Dhabi Health Services Company "SEHA". I recently started making travel videos and look forward to traveling around the world photographing the lives and cultures of countries and creating videos of his travel experiences, and I am apart of the Creative Space Studio
